Why this can move your numbers fast

Type 2 diabetes = too much sugar in your system for too long.

Most of that sugar comes from carbs:

  • Bread, pasta, rice
  • Cereal, crackers, chips
  • Juice, soda, sweets
  • Snack bars and baked goods

In your plan we‚Äôll:
