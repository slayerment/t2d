Step 2 · Your intake

Tell me about your real life so your plan fits it.

This takes about 5–7 minutes. Honest, imperfect answers are better than “ideal” ones.

Your intake is what lets me build a 3-week experiment that fits:

  • When you actually wake, work, and sleep
  • What you really eat now (no judgment)
  • How much walking or movement is realistic for you
  • What meds you’re on and what your main goals are

There are no “right” answers here. The goal is to capture your actual baseline so we can change your numbers without blowing up your life.

Be real, not impressive. Your plan will be better for it.

What this intake covers

Keeps it to the essentials
  • Basics: Name, email, where you’re based
  • Your diagnosis & meds: T2D or pre-diabetes, what you’re taking, any history of lows
  • Your routine: Wake time, work hours, bedtime, family rhythm
  • Your food: Usual breakfast / lunch / dinner, snacks, foods you love & foods you refuse
  • Movement: How much you walk now, what feels realistic most days
  • Your goals: Less meds, lower numbers, weight, energy, or all of the above

If you don’t know an exact number (like your latest A1C), it’s okay to give your best estimate or skip it.
