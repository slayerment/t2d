Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Happy Healing (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website, use our intake forms, or purchase a plan or product related to our type 2 diabetes lifestyle experiments.

1. Who we are

Happy Healing is an online service that offers education and personalized lifestyle experiments for people with type 2 diabetes and pre-diabetes. We are not a medical practice and do not provide medical care, diagnosis, or treatment.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

We collect information you choose to give us, including:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address)
  • Basic demographic information you share (e.g., approximate age range, gender, etc.)
  • Intake form responses, such as:
    • Your current eating patterns and food preferences
    • Your schedule, routines, and lifestyle constraints
    • High-level health information you voluntarily share (e.g., “type 2 diabetes,” medications you take, goals)
  • Information you send via email or other communication channels

2.2 Payment information

When you purchase a plan or product, payments are processed securely by a third-party provider (such as Stripe or another payment processor). We do not see or store your full credit card number. We may receive limited information from the payment provider such as:

  • Confirmation that payment was successful
  • Last four digits of your card and card type
  • Billing country and basic billing details

2.3 Usage data and cookies

Like most websites, we may collect basic technical and usage data automatically, such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Browser type and device type
  • Approximate geographic region (not precise location)
  • Referring pages or links

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how people use the site. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Creating your custom 3-week lifestyle plan based on your intake responses
  • Communicating with you about your purchase, plan, or updates to our services
  • Improving our content, tools, and future versions of the intake and planning system
  • Analyzing aggregated, de-identified trends (e.g., what goals or challenges are most common)
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service

4. How we do not use your information

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your intake details to target unrelated advertising
  • Share your identifiable information with others except as described in this policy

5. When we share your information

We may share limited information with trusted third parties who help us provide our services, such as:

  • Payment processors who handle transactions securely
  • Hosting providers and analytics tools
  • Email service providers we use to communicate with you

These providers are only allowed to use your information to perform services for us and are expected to protect it.

We may also share information if required by law, such as in response to a valid subpoena or court order.

6. Health information and disclaimers

While you may choose to share health-related information with us (e.g., that you have type 2 diabetes or what medications you take), we are not a medical provider and we do not create medical records.

Our site and services are intended for informational and educational purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. You should always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider regarding any questions you have about your health, medications, or treatment.

7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as it is reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including providing your plan, improving our services, and following legal or accounting requirements.

You may request that we delete certain information, subject to any obligations we have to retain it for legal, security, or operational reasons.

8. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we aim to protect your data using appropriate safeguards.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing the personal information we hold about you
  • Requesting corrections to inaccurate information
  • Requesting deletion of certain information
  • Opting out of non-essential email communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before acting on your request.

10. Third-party links

Our website may link to other sites (for example, payment providers, educational resources, or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

11. Children’s privacy

Our services are designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top. In some cases, we may provide additional notice (such as email or a notice on the website).
